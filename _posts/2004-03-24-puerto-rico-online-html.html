I gave the new version of Puerto Rico Online a try last night. Overall I was very impressed. The interface isn't
great, but is still much better than the one at
BSW. What I like the most about it is how
quickly I was able to get online and jump into a game. This is because this
version supports computer AI players. Oddly enough, and I could be wrong here
but I'm pretty sure I saw this happen, human players can join in after a game
has started and replace a computer player. The AI appears to be fairly weak,
but I suspect it will improve over time.
