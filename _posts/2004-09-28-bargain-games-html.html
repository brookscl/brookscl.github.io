There are a few good deals out there that are worth looking into:

  • ToysRUs is still offering Roller Coaster Tycoon for a paltry $5.97 plus shipping. Well worth it. You should probably stay away from Austin Powers Pictionary though.
  • GameFest is running a blowout sale with some decent titles at some decent prices. Some good deals include Adam & Eva and Amun Re. Make sure you compare prices with Boards & Bits - they generally have the best prices on the web.
