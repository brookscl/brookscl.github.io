Step 1: Go buy a Sticheln deck.

Step 2: Go download this comprehensive set of rules for games that can be played with a Sticheln deck. The book is big, but why not load it onto your eReader or tablet device?

I generally pack several card games when we go on trips, but with this I can pack a single deck with some tokens (Die Sieben Siegel must be supported) and play many of our favorites.
