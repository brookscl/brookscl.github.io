What I’m Reading

  • Tao of Seneca – Free PDFs – This is more of a pointer if you want to get a taste of what the stoic thing is all about.
  • Finished reading Think Python and I didn’t think it was very good. I wouldn’t use it for a CS class. There are better intro books and I’ll try to mention some later.

What I’m Listening To

  • Jeff Tweedy - Together at Last – Yes, I’m a Tweedy fanboy going back to the 80s so I’ll always listen to his new stuff. I love his acoustic sets because I can pick apart his songs and find new ways to play them on the guitar.
  • First Listen: Offa Rex, ‘The Queen of Hearts’ – Thought I’d like this more with The Decemberists involved, but alas no.

What I’m Contemplating

  • I like programming again
    • How You Can Make Serious Money By Learning To Code On Vacation – Is this the developer of tomorrow?
    • 79 Websites To Get Freelance Jobs Fast – They went scrounging for this article and I can tell you some of the sites they point to are unlikely to help you find a job. I’m not even sure they are all active. Still, I’m doing some deep diving here to learn more about this economy.
  • I went from sedentary academic to 100-mile marathon runner—thanks to the science of self-control – I didn’t see a lot of science discussed in this article, other than “self discipline means you can run 100 miles!”

  • The Golden Age of Bailing – I’ve noticed this, have you?

What I’m Playing

  • Played the classic Catan with Geoff and Mike. I don’t love this game but will always play it. Always seems to be a slog, even when I’m winning.
