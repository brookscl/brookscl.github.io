Mayfair Games issued a press release
today announcing that
Microsoft will publish an online version of
Settlers of Catan as a premium game
service at MSN Zone. Overall I think this is a positive
thing - more exposure means more publishers and potentially more (and better
games). I do worry about what this will do to some of the free online version
such as Settlers 3D and
BSW.
