Google Maps continues to get even more interesting. They recently added satellite imagery to the maps, adding a much-needed feature that helps bring context to online maps. Google still needs improvement though: the map address database still has some significant omissions and inaccuracies.

One interesting past-time: looking at images of the different places I've lived during my lifetime.
