What I’m Contemplating

  • Rands Information Practices – Great tips on managing your information diet as well as stealing precious seconds (that add up to minutes then hours) in your daily workflows.
  • Our Roth Regrets // The Roth IRA Strategy We Wish We’d Built – I still follow the financial independence / early retirement (FIRE) community closely. Some good tips here for those of you in the earlier stages.
  • Why Cheap Beer Is Still Awesome (And 5 You Should Enjoy This Summer) – Put me down for PBR. Important omission: Yuengling.
  • Tom Russell on Hollandspiele plans – I’m starting to really like this company and their creativity (and their prolific publishing schedule). Confession: I haven’t played any of their games yet, so all I know is from reading reviews.
  • “I Was Devastated”: Tim Berners-Lee, the Man Who Created the World Wide Web, Has Some Regrets – I understand his points but I’m not sure a new software package or platform is going to solve the problems.

What I’m Coding

  • I continue to enhance our Keuka Komplishments app. Fun to have a sandbox like this that people are actually using.
  • I’m building a lightweight Reddit clone for my upcoming Microservice Architecture class at Western Oregon. I’m building a monolithic app as a starting point for the class from which we will go through a series of refactorings into microservice architecture.
  • Python Idioms: Multiline Strings – I’m dialed in deeply to the Python community (podcasts, blogs, etc.) in the hopes of dramatically improving my inherent understanding and memory of the language and APIs.

What I’m Listening To

  • First Listen: Lera Lynn, ‘Plays Well With Others’ – This is a collection of collaborations. Easy listening country, and not the pop kind.
  • Even On ‘Passwords,’ Dawes Can’t Hide Its Big, Bleeding Heart – I’m enjoying this one a lot.
