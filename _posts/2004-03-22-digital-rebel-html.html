A while back I raised the quandry "should I get a Digital Rebel". Well,
about a week ago Julie and I took the dive and we are starting to get some
nice results. I spent some time Sunday morning playing with the automatic and
manual settings - this camera operates just like the old Eos Elan but much
better. Of course the amazing part is the instant feedback from settings to a
viewable image - a much better way to learn and adapt.

This was taken with the zoom lens.
